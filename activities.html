Tree Ropes

Ascend into the treetops at Out and Up's Tree Ropes assault course. Complete with tightrope and zipline, this activity is perfect for those wanting to push themselves to the limit. Two hour sessions are available to book for groups up to 35.





Abseiling

Out and Up offers a range of abseiling courses, from treetop descents to cliff paths, there’s a course for everyone. Although not open all year round, these abseiling courses are perfect for a couple of hours of adventure for groups of up to 35.





Bike Scramble

The Bike Scramble has trails to suit riders of all abilities from complete beginners to World Class athletes. Many of the trails are all weather so riders can enjoy them even in the winter months. Our expert coaches will ensure that you can make the most of our Bike Scramble routes.





Rafting

We are proud to offer adventure all year with our fantastic white water rafting course on the River Tummel and River Orchy. We offer half-day (morning or afternoon) or a full day of activities and you can be guaranteed a thrilling experience that you won't forget!

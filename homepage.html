About Us

'Out and Up' is a company that runs team-building exercises for young adults.

With an activity centre which includes an assault course, a bike trail, a café, and more there's plenty to do in a day.

The activity centre is designed to cater for all ages, especially adults between the age of 17 and 23.

To find out more about the activities that we have to offer, visit the activities page, or come and visit us for the day.
